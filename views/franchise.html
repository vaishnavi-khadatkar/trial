FAQ's

What are the preschool hours?
Our preschool operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
What is the teacher-to-student ratio?
We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio of 1:8 to ensure personalized attention.
Do you provide meals and snacks?
Yes, we provide healthy meals and snacks throughout the day.
What curriculum do you follow?
We follow a play-based curriculum designed to foster development and learning through exploration.
Are the teachers qualified?
All our teachers are highly qualified with degrees in early childhood education and extensive experience in childcare.
What is the process for enrollment?
To enroll your child, please fill out the application form on our website and schedule a visit to our preschool.
Do you offer part-time or full-time programs?
Yes, we offer both part-time and full-time programs to accommodate different schedules.
How do you handle allergies and dietary restrictions?
We take allergies and dietary restrictions very seriously. Please inform us of any specific needs, and we will ensure your child is accommodated.
How do you communicate with parents?
We communicate with parents through regular newsletters, parent-teacher conferences, and a secure online portal where you can access updates about your child.
What extracurricular activities do you offer?
We offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including music, art, dance, and outdoor play.
How do you handle discipline?
We use positive reinforcement and redirection techniques to guide childrenâ€™s behavior. Our goal is to create a nurturing and supportive environment.
What is your policy on sick children?
Children who are sick should stay home to prevent the spread of illness. If a child becomes sick at preschool, we will notify the parents immediately.
Can parents visit the preschool?
Yes, parents are welcome to visit. We encourage you to schedule a visit to see our facilities and meet our staff.
What safety measures are in place?
Our preschool is equipped with secure entry systems, and all staff are trained in emergency procedures. We also conduct regular safety drills.
Is there a nap time?
Yes, we have a designated nap time in the afternoon to ensure children get the rest they need.
